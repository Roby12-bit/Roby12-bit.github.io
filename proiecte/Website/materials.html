Cement bags

Building material in the form of a fine powder, obtained by grinding clinker and which, in contact with water, takes hold and Strengthens

Metal nails

A piece of metal consisting of a sharp rod and an end called flower, with which different parts are fixed together in a wall or wood to serve as a support

Bricks

Artificial building material, in the shape of a piece obtained from a mixture of clay, sand and water, or from other materials, dried in the sun or burned in the oven.

AAC
(autoclaved aerated concrete)

Type of lightweight concrete, composed of a mixture of cement, lime, sand and water, to which a foaming agent is added.

Plasterboard

Cardboard used for the construction of partitions from plasterboard, ceilings, cladding and other interior design with plasterboard.

Reinforced concrete

Composite material formed by pouring concrete over a reinforcement made up of steel bars and wires, sometimes also polymers, which are it transforms by drying into a hard and rigid compound.

Metal beams

Beams are resistance elements in the form of straight bars horizontal or inclined and have a long length in relation to the dimensions transverse, being used in constructions with openings and loads Large

OSB Boards
(Oriented Strand Board)

Oriented wood chip board, multiple layers of wood chips, different sizes and shapes being glued with synthetic resins and pressed hot to form an OSB board
